Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use Stellaris Builder, a private open-source project.

1. Data Collection

I collect personal data that you voluntarily provide when you register for an account or create your star empire. This may include:

  • Commander Name (Username)
  • Email address
  • Login credentials (encrypted)

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The data is stored and used exclusively for the functionality of the Stellaris Builder application, such as:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Saving your progress and settings

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure cloud database (Firebase) provided by Google. I implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that Stellaris Builder is a private project and not intended for storing sensitive or production-level data.

4. User Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the data stored about you
  • Request correction or completion of your data
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

5. Third-Party Services

I use Firebase (a Google service) for data storage and authentication. Google may process and store data on servers located outside the European Union.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact me at jowieja22@gmail.com.
